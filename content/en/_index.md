# GbbOptimizer — Manual

Welcome to the **GbbOptimizer** documentation — a solar energy storage optimizer.

GbbOptimizer analyzes PV production forecasts, household consumption, energy prices, and battery state to automatically control the charging and discharging of an energy storage system. It supports Victron, Deye, GoodWe, Afore, Hinen, and Sofar inverters.
