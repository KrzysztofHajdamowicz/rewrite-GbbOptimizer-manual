# Integrations

GbbOptimizer supports a wide range of inverters and connection methods. Choose the section that matches your hardware setup.

## Sections

- **[Inverters]({{< relref "inverters" >}})** — Brand-specific setup guides (Victron, Deye, Goodwe, Afore, Hinen, Sofar)
- **[Connection Methods]({{< relref "connection-methods" >}})** — How to connect your inverter to GbbOptimizer (Solarman, DeyeCloud, GbbConnect2, DongleDirect)
- **[Home Assistant]({{< relref "home-assistant" >}})** — Mosquitto bridge, SolarAssistant, automations, chart embedding
- **[Other Platforms]({{< relref "other" >}})** — Evcc.io, Tuya, Supla

> [!WARNING]
> **Solarman issues:** Due to ongoing connectivity problems with Solarman, it is recommended to add DeyeCloud as an additional or alternative connection method.
